Common Crop Diseases

Apple Scab

Symptoms: Dark, velvety spots on leaves and fruits.

Medicine: Mancozeb or Captan.

Favourable Environment: Wet, cool spring weather.

Citrus Canker

Symptoms: Raised brown lesions on leaves and fruit with yellow halos.

Medicine: Copper-based bactericides.

Favourable Environment: Warm, wet, and windy conditions.

Bacterial Leaf Spot (Bell Pepper)

Symptoms: Small, water-soaked, dark lesions on leaves and fruits.

Medicine: Copper fungicides and streptomycin.

Favourable Environment: Warm, wet, and humid conditions.

Brown Rust (Wheat)

Symptoms: Small, round, brown pustules on leaf surfaces.

Medicine: Propiconazole or Tebuconazole fungicides.

Favourable Environment: Moist conditions and moderate temperatures.

Northern Corn Leaf Blight

Symptoms: Long, elliptical grayish-green lesions on lower leaves.

Medicine: Fungicides like Azoxystrobin or Mancozeb.

Favourable Environment: Cool temperatures with high humidity.

False Smut (Rice)

Symptoms: Green spore balls turning black on rice grains.

Medicine: Carbendazim or Propiconazole.

Favourable Environment: High nitrogen levels and humid conditions.

Anthracnose (Mango)

Symptoms: Dark, sunken spots on leaves, stems, and fruit.

Medicine: Copper oxychloride or Carbendazim sprays.

Favourable Environment: Warm and wet weather.

Phytophthora Blight

Symptoms: White moldy growth on fruit, stem cankers, and fruit rot.

Medicine: Metalaxyl or Mancozeb.

Favourable Environment: Excessive moisture and poor drainage.

Ripe Fruit Rot (Chilli)

Symptoms: Sunken, dark lesions on mature fruits with fungal growth.

Medicine: Carbendazim or Hexaconazole.

Favourable Environment: Warm, humid conditions during fruiting stage.

Purple Blotch (Onion)

Symptoms: Oval, sunken purple spots on leaves with yellow margins.

Medicine: Mancozeb or Iprodione.

Favourable Environment: Warm and humid conditions with poor air circulation.

White Blister (Broccoli)

Symptoms: White, blister-like pustules on leaves and pods; deformed heads.

Medicine: Metalaxyl or Mancozeb; use disease-free seeds.

Favourable Environment: Cool, moist conditions with frequent fog or irrigation.
